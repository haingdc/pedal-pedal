I took a train from Vientiane to Luang Prabang in 2 hours. First thing I see, the riverside. It was amazing Mekong flow peacefully. Look toward the other side of the river, long long spans mountains with brown tall trees. The ships run slowly. They serve as restaurants and sightseeing boat tours. I sat below an old tree patiently, ordered a cup of coffee watching beauty landscape.

In the afternoon, when the sudden rain had gone, I took for a walk. In a while, I realized every houses have all tiles of roof. Event popular ones like 7-eleven, Lotteria, Amazon cafe,... People planed a lot of flowers and trees. Now, I mean at that moment, sunshined to every houses, the rain made a fresh on my mind. Life is much easier with a garden in front of your door.

Next day I walked down in the long and winding market. They sell all kind of foods, fashion, sourvenir. Street food look tasty. Stoped to buy sticky rice. The woman wrapped around by banana leafs in the old way. All the day, I spent time to walk and happy to have chance try a local coffee with beauty black colour, smell good, the taste is sour, warm. It was relax to see group of old men drank coffee with cake, some beautiful Laos girls had breakfast. Wow! It simply my lucky day.

Luang Prabang is famous about the bluely romance Kuang Si waterfall. It's the place great for trekking. How hot it can be but it was not a problem. I stepped inside the forest, breath much fresh air I can. It was glad to put your feet into water. Then small fishes call each other massage for you. Thank you so much Mother nature.
